DREAMS

Dreams are series of emotions,thoughts or imagination that we encounter when we are asleep and uncouncious.Dreams can make us happy, sad, scared, couragious, strong, weak, hopeful or even lonely.Dreams are so common since everyone has probably experienced it in their sleep.

Interesting Facts about Dreams

  • Everyone Dreams -This includes women, men and children in general.
  • We have control over some dreams.
  • Most dreams are common to everyone and are experienced globally.
  • People with visual disability can dream visually.
  • Generally most dreams contains negative feelings such as frustration, anger,sadness or even loneliness.
  • Animals also have the ability to dream normally as human beings.
  • Female have a different way of dreaming compared to men.
  • Some dreams do are not coloured,they normally appear in form of black and white.

Types of Dreams

  • Daydreams ; This type of dream appears when one is awake but his or her mind is uncounscious,probable deep in thoughts or imagining things hence 0 concentration on whatever is happening around hi or her.
  • Lucid Dreams ; This dream occurs when a person is awake and has an idea of what he or she is dreaming and this type of dream gives the dreamer the chance to actively perticipate in his or her dream and even make decisions conerning that particular dream.
  • Nightmares ; This are dreams that appear to be real and are usually scary such that the person dreaming wakes up in panic,with shouts and even screams.
  • Recurring Dreams ; These types of dreams repeat themselves,it can be afer every two days,five weeks or even months.

Reasons for Dreaming

  • To represent our desires, wishes and goals that tend to be beyond us or out of our reach at that particular moment of our life.
  • To translate various signals from our brains to our bodies when we are deep asleep.
  • To gather, process and interprate information or activities carried out during the the day.

Functions of Dreaming
  1. It incorporates past experience and blend it to suit our future and the challenges we are likely to encounter later on as we work towards achieving our goals and objectives.
  2. It enhances daily learning reconsiling long-term memories of how to do things and it also provide a forum for deleting unwanted information.
  3. Dreams enhances the brain development of young babies since it excercises and stimulates the minds of the children.
